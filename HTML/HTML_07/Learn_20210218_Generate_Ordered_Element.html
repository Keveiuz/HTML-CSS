Emmit: Generate serial elements

1. Use '$' to represent Serial Number

2. Use '*N' to represent the amount of serial element you want to produce

*Syntax: '.Emmit_$*10', 10 'Emmit_N'-class div, range 1 to 10

<div class="Emmit_1"></div>
<div class="Emmit_2"></div>
<div class="Emmit_3"></div>
<div class="Emmit_4"></div>
<div class="Emmit_5"></div>
<div class="Emmit_6"></div>
<div class="Emmit_7"></div>
<div class="Emmit_8"></div>
<div class="Emmit_9"></div>
<div class="Emmit_10"></div>


3. Use '{}' pairs to indicate the content in the tag

*Syntax: 'a.Shortcut_${No.$}*5', five hyperlink with class 'Shortcut_N' and content 'No.N' range from 1 to 10

<a href="" class="Shortcut_1">No.1</a>
<a href="" class="Shortcut_2">No.2</a>
<a href="" class="Shortcut_3">No.3</a>
<a href="" class="Shortcut_4">No.4</a>
<a href="" class="Shortcut_5">No.5</a>
